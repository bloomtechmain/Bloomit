# Bloomit ERP — New Customer Data Onboarding Guide

Welcome to Bloomit. Before your system goes live, we need to collect your existing
business data so it can be loaded into your account. This guide tells you exactly
what information to prepare, section by section.

---

## How the Onboarding Process Works

```
Step 1 — Gather your data (using this guide)
Step 2 — Fill in the provided Excel templates
Step 3 — Submit to your onboarding specialist
Step 4 — We load the data and verify it
Step 5 — You review and sign off
Step 6 — Go live
```

You do not need to enter everything at once. The sections marked **Must Have (Go-Live)**
are required before you can start. The rest can be added after you are up and running.

## What You Do NOT Need to Prepare

The following are generated automatically by the system — do not try to include them
in your submitted data:

- Employee numbers (auto-generated)
- Quote numbers, PO numbers, payslip IDs
- Timestamps and audit logs
- System notifications and email logs
- Password hashes (users are invited by email and set their own passwords)

---

## Tips Before You Start

**Use your most recent month-end as the go-live date.**
All opening balances (bank accounts, loans) should reflect the closing balance
at the end of the month before you go live. For example, if your go-live date is
1 April 2024, use your 31 March 2024 balances.

**Get your data from these sources:**
- Bank accounts → latest bank statements
- Employees → HR files / payroll software export
- Vendors → existing supplier list or accounting software
- Assets → fixed asset register or depreciation schedule
- Loans → bank loan statements
- Receivables / Payables → accounts receivable and accounts payable listings

**One designated person per section is best.**
Assign one person who owns each dataset — the person most familiar with it.
Mixed-source data causes inconsistencies.
